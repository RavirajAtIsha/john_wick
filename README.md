## The Problem

Isha Foundation faces persistent online misinformation - false allegations about land encroachment, misleading narratives about practices, and distorted facts about resolved legal matters. These attacks spread rapidly across platforms while manual responses are slow, inconsistent, and can't match the scale of the problem.

When supporters encounter these false claims in WhatsApp groups or social conversations, they often lack immediate access to documented counter-evidence, leaving misinformation unchallenged.

---

## The Solution

John Wick is an automated system that detects negative content in real-time, analyzes it using AI, matches it to official proof documents from government sources, and generates professional counter-narratives, that can be deployed as targeted ads **directly alongside the same article by detecting its content using AI**, or shared instantly via WhatsApp.

---

## How It Works

**1. Web Monitoring**
- Playwright scraper continuously monitors Google, YouTube, news sites for negative keywords
- Avoids bot detection through human-like browser behavior
- Captures article content, metadata, and publishing information

**2. AI Analysis**
- Gemini AI processes content for sentiment, topic classification, and severity scoring
- VADER provides nuanced emotional analysis
- Categorizes articles by threat level (Low, Medium, High, Critical)

**3. Intelligent Matching**
- Topic-based algorithm matches articles to proof documents
- AI understands context, not just keywords
- 87% coverage achieved through semantic understanding

**4. Counter-Ad Generation**
- System creates professional responses with headlines and body text
- Includes government proof images and official documents
- WhatsApp sharing capability built-in

**5. Deployment**
- Counter-ads placed as targeted ads on platforms where negativity appears
- Supporters can share documented responses instantly in conversations
- Truth reaches audiences at the same time as misinformation

## Future Evolution

### Multi-Platform Expansion

The system will expand to comprehensively monitor and respond across all major platforms:

- **YouTube:** Video content analysis, comment monitoring, targeted pre-roll ads on negative videos
- **Twitter/X:** Real-time tweet monitoring, viral thread detection, promoted tweet responses
- **Reddit:** Community discussion tracking, comment analysis across relevant subreddits
- **Facebook & Instagram:** Post and story monitoring, targeted ad placement in user feeds
- **News Aggregators:** Track mentions across major news platforms and content aggregators

### Multilingual Intelligence

False narratives spread in multiple languages. The system will expand to detect and counter misinformation in:

- Tamil, Hindi, Telugu, Kannada, Malayalam
- Regional language social media and news sites
- Automated translation of counter-narratives to match source language

### Reinforcement Learning for Smarter Matching

Current system achieves 87% coverage through topic-based AI matching. Reinforcement learning will push this further:

- **Learning from feedback:** System learns which counter-narratives work best for specific types of allegations
- **Pattern recognition:** Identifies new forms of misinformation before they're explicitly categorized
- **Dynamic weighting:** Automatically adjusts matching algorithm based on effectiveness data
- **Self-improvement:** Coverage percentage continuously improves as the model learns from each new case

**Goal:** Push coverage from 87% to 95%+ through continuous machine learning, moving beyond rule-based keyword systems to true AI understanding.

### Advanced Analytics

- **Threat heatmaps:** Geographic and temporal visualization of attack patterns
- **Trend prediction:** Identify emerging narratives before they go viral
- **Campaign detection:** Recognize coordinated misinformation efforts across platforms
- **Impact measurement:** Track effectiveness of counter-ad placements and optimize strategy

### Chrome Extension Demo Tool

Browser extension will inject counter-ad overlays directly on negative article pages, providing visual demonstration of how targeted ads would appear to readers. Perfect for stakeholder presentations showing real-world deployment scenarios.
